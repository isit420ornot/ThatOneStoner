Capstone Project One

I openly admit that I don't live an Instagram worthy existence… Especially since the camera-friendly façade of a life came grinding to a halt… leading me to move back home… after 20 years gone… to focus on my mental health…

I probably still have a long way to go before I can use a word like healed… but I can finally say that I’m excited about where I am… and confident that I now at least know which way I’m heading… as I continue to hone my web development skills…

Unlike other projects from the past… I’m okay if ThatOneStoner.com doesn’t end up breaking any molds… I’m now learning… or better yet… finally willing to admit that… my projects from the past were obsessive effort to work through failed areas of life… and not truly an attempt to generate art…

This time… I will attempt to keep my work accessible to as broad of an audience as I can… even if I continue to commit to the pothead theme while exploring the world of online education… I’m still trying to escape the realm of the normals after all…

The Task At Hand...

For this assignment... I'm supposed to create a landing page with a sign-up form... still using nothing but the HTML and CSS that’s been covered by the course so far... once the user submits the form… they are supposed to be taken to a thank you page…

Again… since this form building is just for practice… the inputted information goes nowhere… since we’ve to learn the back-end languages that deal with submitted form data…

Because of this… I’m going to stray a little from the scope of the project’s breakdown… I’m going to let MailChimp take care of the form submit features for me… and let PayPal take control of the functionality of the “Donate” image button…

Thank you for reading this far... and I appreciate… in advance… any efforts that any of you make to play along... even if that just means hitting a bong while staring at all the colorful pages from time to time…

With all of that said... I give you the task at hand!!!
